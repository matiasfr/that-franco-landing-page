About

That Franco is an underground UKG and house music producer known for blending classic UK rave energy with modern production. With high-energy beats, basslines, and an authentic garage vibe, That Franco brings the spirit of the 90s rave scene to today’s dance floors. His performances are an ode to the underground – bold, expressive, and unapologetically fun.
